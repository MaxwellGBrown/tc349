Card Sorting -- NASA Moon Website

Process & Findings

During the 4 card sortings (including mine) of the NASA moon website, the following similarities were found:

  • Many sorters moved the LADEE information from any section of NASA's site to some sort of "projects" section.
  • Sections about moon songs, any sort of pictures, and any sort of video tended to have been grouped into a "media" section.
  • Information about Neil Armstrong, past missions, and the general "About the Moon" information was most likely grouped with "History/Info".
  • "Strategic Partners", "Get Involved", and "People" from the NASA site was often organized together. Also included is the NASALunar Science Forum, and education materials originally from "About the Moon". I personally labeled this grouping "Community".
  • Many common Outliers between sorters were from the "Moon Toolkit" section from the website. Many items linked to LADEE information were decided on as outliers. I think this is perhaps because the sortees didn't understand the purpose of these cards and how they relate to LADEE.

These were the common things found during the card sorting activity. The first two times the card sort was done I did a poor job at explaining what exactly was being done, so my sortees asked a lot of questions, and didn't quite understand exactly what they were doing, or were overwhelmed by the number of cards.
