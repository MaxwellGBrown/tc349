Card Sorting -- My Website

About My Website

For a while now I've played around with the idea of hosting my own website to include my resume, some sort of portfolio, and other information about myself/what I've been up to. Whether to create just a professional website, or an all encompassing website with professional material included is still up for debate. Regardless, now seems as good a time as any to start!

Process & Findings

During the 4 card sortings (including mine) of my website, the following similarities were found:

  • Sorters agreed that the portfolio and the resume should be grouped together.
  • Sorters were split on whether the main page should be populated with "About Me" info or with the "What-I've-Been-Up-To" info.
  • Sorters agreed that a section for sharing recorded music of mine should be a main section of the website.
  • Few/no outliers were selected by a sortee.

This sort was much less comprehensive than the NASA sort. I had a much smaller amount of information than the NASA sort, and the content was published after doing the NASA Moon card sort so I think that the material was much more abbreviated and less ambiguous. Also, there wasn't any content that wasn't understood by the sorters unlike in the NASA sort.
